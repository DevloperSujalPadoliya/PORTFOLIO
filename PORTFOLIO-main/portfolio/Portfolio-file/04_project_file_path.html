Delicious Veg Biryani Recipe

This Veg Biryani is a flavorful and Easy to make dish that brings joy to every meal. Perfeft for weekends!
It's rich in protein and Vitamin B12, and tastes best when served hot.



Ingredients

  • 2 Cups Basmati Rice
  • 1 Cup Mixes Vegetables
  • 1 Onion (sliced)
  • 1 Tsp Biryani masala
  • Salt,Oil,Mint,Coriander

How to Cook

  1. Wash and soak rice for 30 minutes
  2. soute onion and vegetables
  3. Add spices and cook for 5 mins
  4. Mix with cooked rice and simmer
  5. Serve hot with raita
